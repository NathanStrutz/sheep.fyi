Bring your class
to sheep.fyi.

Teaching a class at SHEEP? I'll host a small, fast website for it — your syllabus, notes, and links, all at your own subdomain. It's free, it stays out of your way, and updating it is as easy as editing a text file.

How it works
04 steps
01
Send me your class
Email me what your class covers and any materials you already have — a syllabus, notes, a doc, even a rough outline. Don't worry about formatting.
02
Pick a subdomain
Your site lives at its own address, like your-class.sheep.fyi. Tell me what you'd like it to be.
03
I build & publish it
I turn your materials into a clean, plain-markdown site and put it live. No accounts, no ads, no clutter — just your content, fast.
04
Update it anytime
Need a change? Send it over, or learn the markdown yourself — it's just text. Edits go live in minutes.

What you get

  • Your own subdomain at sheep.fyi
  • A clean, fast, mobile-friendly site
  • Written in plain markdown — easy to edit
  • Free hosting, no ads, no tracking clutter
  • As many pages as your class needs

What to include

  • Class name and a one-line description
  • Grade or age range you teach
  • Your preferred subdomain
  • Any materials — syllabus, notes, links, schedule
  • How you'd like to handle updates (I'll do it, or you will)
Ready when you are

Let's get your class online.

Send me the details above and I'll follow up with your subdomain and a first draft. No commitment — reach out and we'll figure out the rest.
